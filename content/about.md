I'm Massimiliano Aroffo, engineering leader with 25 years in software development who deliberately returned to hands-on technical work to master AI-assisted development and cloud architecture firsthand.

After building and scaling teams as CTO, VP Engineering, and CISO across multiple companies, I recognized that the shift in AI-assisted development required deep technical immersion, not observation from the leadership layer. I now bring a strategic mindset to code: designing high-performance systems in Go, building AI-augmented development workflows, and applying Platform Engineering principles to reduce cognitive load.

My leadership background informs my technical work: I build systems with team scalability in mind, document institutional knowledge as executable patterns, and optimize for developer experience alongside raw performance.

Currently working as **Principal Software Engineer at [HikmaAI](https://hikmaai.io/)**, building responsible AI governance and security tooling, and as **Cloud Engineer & Architect at [Wishew](https://wishew.com/)**, where I built high-performance infrastructure for a social crowdfunding platform.
